## 🎨 Pattern Practice Problems

### 🟢 Easy
1. **Square of Stars**  
   Print a 4×4 square made of stars.  
   ```
   ****
   ****
   ****
   ****
   ```

2. **Right-Angled Triangle**  
   Print a triangle of stars with 5 rows.  
   ```
   *
   **
   ***
   ****
   *****
   ```

---

### 🟠 Medium
3. **Number Triangle**  
   Print numbers in a triangle pattern.  
   ```
   1
   12
   123
   1234
   12345
   ```

4. **Inverted Star Triangle**  
   Print stars in reverse order.  
   ```
   *****
   ****
   ***
   **
   *
   ```

---

### 🔴 Hard
5. **Pyramid Pattern**  
   Print a centered pyramid of stars.  
   ```
       *
      ***
     *****
    *******
   *********
   ```
